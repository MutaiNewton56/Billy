  • Shoot Date: 15 June, 2024

Capturing Beauty in Simplicity

This project involved a mix of lifestyle portraits and product photography to showcase handcrafted items in a natural and artistic way. The goal was to highlight texture, emotion, and the personal story behind each piece.

By blending editorial-style setups with authentic storytelling, each shot was carefully framed to balance creativity and clarity. The final visuals were used for social media campaigns, online stores, and branding materials.
